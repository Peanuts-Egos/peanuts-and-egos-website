Peanuts & Egos: Touchdowns, Triumphs, and Turmoil in the German Football League

"There's a sucker at every poker table, and if you can't figure out who it is after the first hand, it's you."

Ever wonder what happens when American football dreams meet European reality? Join former NFL Europe player and German Football League coach Denauld Brown for the untold stories of football's global journey. From phantom cities in Germany to packed London stadiums, we're your insider's guide to international football's evolution.

No PR spin. Just real stories from the trenches of the game's first global experiment.

Episodes featuring:

  • Behind-the-scenes tales from NFL Europe
  • Cultural clashes and unexpected triumphs
  • The poker-like strategy of cross-cultural leadership
  • X's and O's of global football politics

The peanuts are small, but the egos? They span continents.
